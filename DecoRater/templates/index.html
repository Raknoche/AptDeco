Welcome to DecoRater. This app is designed to automatically assess the quality of furniture images for AptDeco.com.

Click or drag a jpg, png, or gif below to upload your images. After the files upload, click "Submit" to see the results. The website will automatically order the images from highest quality to lowest quality, and assign a five star rating to each picture. The results will also indicate if an image is suitable for display on the front page of an AptDeco furniture listing. If none of the images are suitable for display on the front page of a listing, a warning will be displayed with suggest improvements.
