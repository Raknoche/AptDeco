Welcome to DecoRater. This app is designed to automatically assess the quality of furniture images for AptDeco.com.


Use DecoRater with these three simple steps:

1. Upload your images by dragging them to the box below. Alternatively, click on the box to select files from your computer. Each image will indicate when it's been successfully uploaded. (jpg, png, or gif formats accepted)

2. Click "Submit" after your images have uploaded.

3. See your results! We'll order your images from highest to lowest quality, and assign 0-5 star rating to each picture. The results will also indicate if an image is suitable for display on the front page of an AptDeco furniture listing. If none of your images are suitable for display on the front page of a listing, a warning will be displayed with suggested improvements to the pictures.
